Everything within earshot.

Orbiton is not a chatbot. It is a command layer between your voice and your operating system.

🎤

Wake Word Activation

Say "TOKYO" and Orbiton snaps to attention. No buttons. No clicks. Just your voice cutting through the noise.

🧠

Neuro-Link Intelligence

Built-in NLP handles homophones, spoken math, and context routing. "Exambored" becomes "exam mode." "Two times two" becomes 2*2.

🔒

Zero Cloud, Zero Telemetry

Everything runs locally. Your voice never leaves your machine. No API keys to manage. No privacy policy to dread.

⚡

System Native

Open VS Code projects, launch Chrome searches, navigate files, check weather, track flights — all through OS-level integration.

🗣️

Edge TTS Voices

Orbiton talks back using neural text-to-speech. Aria, Jenny, or any Edge voice you prefer. It sounds human because it is.

🐍

Python-Powered & Hackable

Written in pure Python. Add commands in minutes. The entire architecture is a single file away from your next great idea.
